# Product FAQs

## General Questions

### What is your product?
Our product is an AI-powered customer service platform that helps businesses automate support, reduce response times, and improve customer satisfaction. It combines natural language processing, machine learning, and intelligent routing to provide 24/7 automated support while seamlessly escalating complex issues to human agents.

### Who is this product for?
Our platform is designed for:
- Small to medium-sized businesses looking to scale their customer support
- E-commerce companies handling high volumes of customer inquiries
- SaaS companies wanting to reduce support costs
- Any business that values customer experience and wants to improve response times

### What makes your product different from competitors?
Key differentiators include:
- **Hybrid AI-Human approach**: Seamless handoff between AI and human agents
- **Easy integration**: Works with existing helpdesk systems (Zendesk, Freshdesk, Intercom)
- **Customizable**: Train the AI on your specific products and policies
- **Multi-channel**: Support across email, chat, social media, and phone
- **Analytics**: Deep insights into customer issues and agent performance

## Troubleshooting

### The AI is giving incorrect responses. What should I do?
1. Review the conversation and identify the issue
2. Use the "Correct Response" feature to teach the AI
3. Check if your knowledge base needs updating
4. Verify the intent is properly configured
5. Contact support if the issue persists

The AI learns from corrections and improves over time.

### How do I handle conversations the AI can't resolve?
You can configure escalation rules:
- Automatically route to human agents based on keywords or sentiment
- Allow customers to request human assistance anytime
- Set confidence thresholds for AI responses
- Create custom escalation workflows

### Can I test changes before deploying to customers?
Yes! We provide:
- Sandbox environment for testing
- A/B testing capabilities
- Preview mode for new responses
- Rollback functionality
- Staged deployments
